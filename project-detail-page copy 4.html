Mimosa Walker

Devised and created by Faith Bebbington and Janet Price.

Mimosa pudica or Sensitive Plant is a creeping annual or perennia-flowering plant of the pea / legume family Fabaceae. The sensitive compound leaves quickly fold inward and droop when touched or shaken and re-open a few minutes later.

This sculpture is interactive, gently pull the strings to activate the sensitive plant reaction!
